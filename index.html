How to Convert Files with Fik-Rago

Fik-Rago Precision Image Lab is a next-generation online image converter designed for speed and security. Whether you need to convert PDF to JPG, optimize WEBP for web, or process HEIC photos from iPhone, our tool handles it all within your browser window.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  • Upload: Drag and drop your files or select them manually. We support 120+ formats.
  • Customize: Use our AI Neural Vision to analyze your image or apply manual adjustments like brightness and contrast.
  • Select Format: Choose your target output (JPG, PNG, WEBP, PDF, TIFF, BMP).
  • Secure Download: Click download to save your file. No data is ever uploaded to a server.
